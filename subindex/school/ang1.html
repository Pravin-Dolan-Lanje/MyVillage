Anganwadi No. 1:

Located in the heart of Siregaon Bandh, this center serves children aged 0-6 years, offering early childhood education and nutritional support.

The center operates from 9:00 AM to 1:30 PM on weekdays and is staffed by an Anganwadi worker and a helper.
