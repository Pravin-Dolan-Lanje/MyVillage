About Us

Siregaon is a Village in Arjuni Morgaon Taluka in Gondia District of Maharashtra State, India. It belongs to Vidarbh region . It belongs to Nagpur Division . It is located 70 KM towards South from District head quarters Gondia. 17 KM from Arjuni-Morgaon. 909 KM from State capital Mumbai

Siregaon Pin code is 441702 and postal head office is Navegaon Bandh .

Gudhari ( 5 KM ) , Bhiwkhidaki ( 5 KM ) , Silezari ( 5 KM ) , Bortola ( 6 KM ) , Sawartola ( 8 KM ) are the nearby Villages to Siregaon. Siregaon is surrounded by Arjuni Morgaon Taluka towards South , Lakhani Taluka towards west , Sadak Arjuni Taluka towards North , Lakhandur Taluka towards South .

Pauni , Bhandara , Tirora , Tumsar are the near by Cities to Siregaon.

This Place is in the border of the Gondia District and Bhandara District. Bhandara District Lakhandur is South towards this place .
